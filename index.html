Zombie Tracker
🧟
Finds companies that hit 52-week lows but are bouncing back with verified catalysts. New CEO, earnings beat, government contract, FDA approval, restructuring, activist investor. Real reasons behind the recovery.
The Logic:
1. Find crashed stock (52-week low)
2. Confirm recovery (20%+ bounce)
3. Verify catalyst (real public facts only)
4. Strength score (bounce % + days since catalyst)

Why It Works: When a stock is down 50%+ and bounces, something fundamental changed. Market eventually prices it in. This catches them early.

Signal Categories: New management, earnings beats, government wins, regulatory approval, restructuring, activist moves, debt resolution.
